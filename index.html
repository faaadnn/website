About Us

Control Automation Technology Sdn Bhd (CATSB) is establishing the Academy of Semiconductor (ASEMI) in response to the growing demand for skilled technical talent within Malaysia’s rapidly advancing semiconductor industry. With over 30 years of experience in semiconductor and PCBA assembly equipment, Control Automation Technology (CATSB) has partnered with major capital equipment manufacturers to provide local sales and service support throughout the entire semiconductor packaging process from die-level handling to inspection and packing. Through the Academy of Semiconductor, Control Automation Technology (CATSB) leverages its deep industry expertise to develop job-ready graduates, equipped with both theoretical knowledge and hands-on skills to meet the needs of today’s semiconductor manufacturing environment.

Vision

The vision of the Academy of Semiconductor is to be Malaysia’s leading centre for semiconductor talent development, producing world-class, job-ready professionals who drive innovation, sustainability, and excellence in line with the nation’s vision to be a global semiconductor hub. This vision is closely aligned with the National Semiconductor Strategy (NSS), which supports Malaysia’s aspiration to be among the top global semiconductor players through the development of a highly skilled and industry-ready workforce.

It also complements the objectives of the Rancangan Malaysia Ke-13 (RMK13), which emphasizes the transition towards an innovation-driven, high-income economy. Furthermore, it reflects the principles of Nilai-nilai Madani, by upholding integrity, industriousness, discipline, and respect in every aspect of professional practice, ensuring that graduates not only excel technically but also demonstrate strong ethical values in their careers.

Mission

Our mission is to deliver industry-aligned education that prepares Malaysia’s youth for high-value careers in the semiconductor sector. Through cutting-edge training in both front-end and back-end manufacturing, we combine strong theory with practical, hands-on experience to ensure participants are truly work-ready. Aligned with the National Semiconductor Strategy (NSS), Rancangan Malaysia Ke-13 (RMK13), and the K-Youth Development agenda, we focus on building the next generation of skilled talent.

Our programs bridge the industry readiness gap by equipping fresh graduates and reskilled talent with the competencies required in fab and OSAT environments. Its delivered through immersive on-the-job training (OJT) and applied case studies. We are committed to cultivating professional excellence and strong values, guided by Nilai-Nilai Madani which are Integrity, Competence, Perseverance, Excellence, and Trustworthiness.

Participants are trained to uphold cleanroom protocols, industry standards, and ethical practices while embracing digital innovation and Industry 4.0 tools. Through our Train & Place platform, we directly connect young talent with industry opportunities, supporting K-Youth’s mission of sustainable employability and contributing to Malaysia’s long-term semiconductor workforce pipeline. Together, we are shaping a future-ready youth workforce that drives innovation, strengthens the supply chain, and positions Malaysia as a global semiconductor hub.

Program

Our programs are claimable under Human Resource Development Corperation (HRD Corp).

  • NEXUS Program (Next Gen Engineering Expertise & Upskilling Semiconductor)

    The Semiconductor IC Manufacturing Training Module is developed to prepare participants for technician or operator-level roles within the semiconductor back-end manufacturing industry. This comprehensive training emphasizes both theoretical understanding and hands-on experience in key processes such as IC packaging and assembly. Participants will gain practical skills aligned with real industry practices, ensuring they are job-ready upon completion.

  • Training Modules:
    • Front End Manufacturing Process & Equipment Mantainance
    • Back End Manufacturing Process & Equipment Mantainance
    • Soft Skill
  • Objectives:
    • To equip fresh graduates with job-ready technical knowledge.
    • To develop essential engineering competencies for fab and OSAT environments.
    • To expose participants to industry standards and cleanroom protocols.
    • To foster data-driven problem-solving and process improvement skills.
    • To bridge the industry readiness gap through applied case studies and hands-on sessions.
    • To support talent pipeline development through structured Train & Place delivery
  • Training Structure:
    • Theory : 1 month
    • On-Job-Training (OJT): 3 months with job placement
  • Duration:
    • 4 months
  • Open To:
    • Degree holers
    • Diploma graduates
    • SKM certificate holders (in Electrical, Mechatronics, Materials, and related field)
  • Age:
    • 18 to 30 years old
