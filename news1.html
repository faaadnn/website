ASEMI Officially Launches 1st Batch of Trainees

Published on September 2, 2025

The Academy of Semiconductors (ASEMI) proudly announces the launch of its first cohort of trainees, marking a significant milestone in Malaysia’s journey toward becoming a regional hub for semiconductor innovation.

This program offers hands-on training in both front-end and back-end semiconductor manufacturing, providing young professionals and upskilling workers with real-world industry experience.

ASEMI’s mission is to close the skills gap in the semiconductor sector, preparing graduates for careers in this fast-growing global industry.
